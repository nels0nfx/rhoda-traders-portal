Job Description

Remote Wall Fittings Counter

Position Overview

Rhoda Traders is seeking reliable individuals to work from home as Remote Wall Fittings Counters. This position involves receiving, counting, and reporting on Wall Fittings that are shipped to your location, then coordinating with FedEx for pickup and delivery to our clients.

This is a flexible, work-from-home opportunity that allows you to set your own hours while providing an essential service to our company and clients.

Your Duties to the Company

Receiving Boxes

The company will send to you boxes containing Wall Fittings. These will be delivered directly to your home address.

Counting Items

You are to open the boxes and count the number of Wall Fittings in each box. Accuracy is essential in this role.

Reporting

After counting, you will submit a detailed report to the company through our online portal, documenting the exact number of items in each box.

Coordinating Pickup

A FedEx Agent will come to your location to pick up the boxes. The agent will have the names and addresses of our clients who need the boxes, so you don't need to handle any shipping labels.

No Financial Responsibility

You will not be asked for any fee or taxes because the company has made arrangements for all shipping and handling costs. This is a zero-cost opportunity for you.

The Process

  1. 1

    Application & Approval

    Submit your application through our online portal. Our admin team will review your application and contact you for approval.

  2. 2

    Initial Shipment

    Once approved, we will arrange for the first shipment of Wall Fittings to be delivered to your address.

  3. 3

    Counting & Reporting

    Open the boxes, count the Wall Fittings in each box, and submit your report through your online dashboard.

  4. 4

    Pickup Coordination

    We will notify you when a FedEx Agent will arrive to pick up the boxes for delivery to our clients.

  5. 5

    Payment Processing

    You will receive payment for your services according to our payment schedule, directly to your preferred payment method.

Requirements

  • Reliable internet connection for submitting reports
  • Secure location to receive and store boxes temporarily
  • Ability to count accurately and maintain detailed records
  • Availability to receive deliveries and coordinate with FedEx for pickups
  • Basic computer skills for using our online reporting system
  • Attention to detail and commitment to accuracy
  • Reliable communication skills
