Frequently Asked Questions

You can apply for a position by visiting our Apply Now page and completing the application form. Make sure to provide all required information and any requested documents.

Our team typically reviews applications within 3-5 business days. If your application is approved, you'll receive an email with instructions on how to proceed.

The main requirements include a secure location to receive and store packages temporarily, reliable internet connection, availability to coordinate with FedEx for pickups, and attention to detail for accurate counting. You can find more information on our Job Description page.

We offer multiple payment methods including direct deposit, PayPal, and other electronic payment options. Payment details will be provided once your application is approved.

Currently, we are accepting applications from the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, and Australia. We plan to expand to more countries in the future.

You can contact our recruitment team at careers@rhodatraders.com with any questions about your application status or the application process.
