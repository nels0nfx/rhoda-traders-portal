Why Work With Us?

Work From Home

Enjoy the flexibility of working from the comfort of your own home. No commuting, no office politics, just a comfortable work environment.

Flexible Hours

Set your own schedule. Work when it's convenient for you, as long as you complete your assigned tasks on time.

Competitive Pay

Earn competitive compensation for your work. Payments are made regularly and reliably.

Simple Tasks

Your primary duty is to count Wall Fittings in boxes and report the numbers. No complicated tasks or specialized skills required.

Your Duties to the Company

1

Receive Boxes

The company will send boxes containing Wall Fittings directly to your location.

2

Count Items

Open the boxes and count the number of Wall Fittings in each box accurately.

3

Submit Reports

Report the counts back to the company through our easy-to-use online portal.

4

Coordinate Pickup

A FedEx Agent will come to your location to pick up the boxes. No fees or taxes will be charged to you.

The FedEx Agent will arrive with the names and addresses of our clients who need the boxes, so you don't need to handle any shipping labels or packaging.
